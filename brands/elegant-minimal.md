# Elegant Minimal Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity
Sophisticated, timeless, and refined. This brand emphasizes elegance through simplicity.

## Color Palette
- Primary: Classic Black (#000000)
- Secondary: Pure White (#ffffff)
- Accent: Soft Gold (#d4af37)
- Neutral: Light Gray (#f5f5f5)
- Text: Off-Black (#1a1a1a)

## Typography
- Headings: Elegant serif fonts (like Playfair Display, Georgia)
- Body: Clean, professional sans-serif with optimal readability
- Ample letter-spacing for headings
- Large, comfortable line-height

## Design Principles
1. **Elegance**: Refined and sophisticated visual presentation
2. **Simplicity**: Less is more - remove unnecessary elements
3. **Quality**: High-quality imagery and careful attention to detail
4. **Balance**: Perfect symmetry and harmonious proportions

## Layout Guidelines
- Centered layouts with generous white space
- Maximum content width: 900px for optimal readability
- Symmetrical designs
- Minimal use of borders and dividers
- Focus on typography hierarchy

## Visual Style
- Use high-quality imagery sparingly
- Subtle, tasteful animations
- Minimalist navigation
- Generous padding between sections
- Use white space as a design element
